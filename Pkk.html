ENGINEER HUNT

"MASSACRE HUNT Extraordinary security measures were taken in the region after Metin Çakır, the manager of the ETİBANK Elazığ Kovancılar Şark Ferrochrome Plant and an engineer, and eight officials were executed by firing squad by PKK militants. Reinforcement gendarme commando units and special operations teams were sent to the area from Diyarbakır. 'The incident occurred the previous evening. A group of terrorists, reported to be 7–8 people, forced workers traveling in trucks and buses to disembark in the Rut Mountain area. They blocked the road with vehicles. They also stopped the vehicles carrying the plant manager and nine officials to conduct ID checks. After setting the workers aside, they brutally massacred the 9 individuals at a distance of 900 meters from the scene. "

ORTABAĞ

"Separatist PKK militants caused the death of 8 citizens and the injury of 15 people, 3 of whom were village guards, by throwing a bomb into a wedding house in Ortabağ village of Hakkâri's Uludere district."

PINARCIK

"Separatist terrorists carried out their bloodiest massacre the previous night in Pınarcık village of Mardin's Ömerli district. Approximately 40 terrorists, wearing special commando uniforms identical to those worn by security forces, massacred 30 people, 16 of whom were children and 6 of whom were women, and wounded 3 children."

SAVUR

"PKK terrorists, who attacked Ormancık and Akyürek villages, did not spare the women and children who sought refuge in a house, committing a crime against humanity by using chemical gas bombs. First, they subjected the house to a hail of automatic weapon fire. Not satisfied with that, they fled after throwing hand grenades and gas bombs onto the terrified and crying women and children. 15 people, mostly innocent children, died at the scene. One child also lost their life on the way while being rushed to the hospital.

JUNE 20, 1987

Pınarcık Massacre

In the Pınarcık village of Mardin's Ömerli district, PKK militants launched a late-night raid targeting civilian households. A total of 30 people were killed, including 16 children and 6 women.

JANUARY 23, 1987

Milan Massacre

This attack took place in the Milan hamlet of Eruh, Siirt. Militants targeted civilians, resulting in the deaths of 8 people. Among the victims was a 3-day-old infant.

JANUARY 23, 1987

Ortabağ Massacre

In the Ortabağ village of Uludere, Şırnak, militants targeted a wedding house by throwing hand grenades into the celebration. The explosion killed 8 civilians.

JUNE 10, 1990

Çevrimli Massacre

Militants raided the Çevrimli village in Güçlükonak, Şırnak. During the raid and the ensuing fires, 27 civilians lost their lives, 12 of whom were children.

DECEMBER 25, 1991

Çetinkaya Store Attack

In broad daylight, the Çetinkaya department store in Bakırköy, Istanbul, was attacked with Molotov cocktails. The resulting fire caused the deaths of 11 people.

MARCH 7, 1987

Açıkyol Massacre

Militants entered the Açıkyol village in Nusaybin, Mardin, and opened fire on civilians. 8 people were killed in the attack, 6 of whom were children.

JUNE 10, 1992

Yuvalı Massacre

A raid on the Yuvalı village in Midyat, Mardin, resulted in the deaths of 10 civilians. This was part of a broader campaign of intimidation.

JULY 5, 1993

Başbağlar Massacre

Approximately 100 militants raided the village of Başbağlar in Erzincan. They executed the village men by firing squad before setting the village on fire. 33 civilians were killed.

JANUARY 21, 1994

Savur Massacre

Simultaneous attacks were carried out in the Ormancık and Akyürek villages of Savur, Mardin. Hand grenades and automatic weapons were used to kill 21 civilians.

MARCH 13, 1999

Mavi Çarşı Massacre

The Mavi Çarşı shopping center in Göztepe, Istanbul, was targeted with Molotov cocktails. 13 civilians died trapped inside the building due to fire.

MARCH 13, 2016

Ankara Kızılay Bombing

A car bomb was detonated near bus stops in the heart of Ankara. The suicide attack killed 36 civilians and injured 344 others.
